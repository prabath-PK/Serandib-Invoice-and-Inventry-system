Project info
A smart and simple POS and billing system designed for small and medium businesses operating online.

It helps you create professional invoices, manage daily sales, and keep full control of your stock.

With built-in inventory tracking, GRN management, and easy account handling, this system ensures your business runs smoothly and efficiently.

Perfect for businesses that want a fast, reliable, and user-friendly solution without unnecessary complexity
